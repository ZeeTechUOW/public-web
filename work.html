Project Craft your Creativity

An interactive e-training with gamification.

Client

Matt Halliwell

Matt Halliwell has been working on an interactive e-learning which focuses on interactivity and team building for his Ph.D. program.

Our client suggest a system which incorporates gamification elements to enhance the experience.

Problem

Current e-learning system

Our client believe that the current e-learning system is focused on individual learning instead of teamwork.

Client looking for a solution:

  • Interactive training system
  • Dynamic module
  • User-friendly towards non-programmer
  • Social media integration
  • Web based platform

Solution

Project Craft your Creativity

Project Craft your Creativity is an e-learning platform which incorporates gamification elements.

With gamification, our solution offers:

  • Engagement and entertainment
  • Challenges
  • Flow or mastery
  • Friendly competition
  • Rewards
  • Knowledge
